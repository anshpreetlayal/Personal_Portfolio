Reading

One of my numerous interests is reading, and I frequently read manga, romance novels, and other types of publications. I can enter into the souls and thoughts of others through books. Any book worth reading is a window into the thoughts of the author's readers. You get better at reading as you read more. I enjoy romantic fiction because it gives me a glimpse into how we perceive love and passion in our daily lives and how we could hope for it in the future. Reading manga is a beautiful experience because it's totally different from reading books and novels. Manga is the Japanese equivalent of comic books, but unlike American comic books, manga serves as more than just a vehicle for narrative. It is distinctive among all other types of art since it can also be used as a canvas for drawing and painting.
